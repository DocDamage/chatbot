# DMAIC

DMAIC is the core Six Sigma improvement roadmap: Define, Measure, Analyze, Improve, and Control.

- Define: project charter, Voice of Customer, CTQ tree, SIPOC, scope, sponsor, and business case.
- Measure: operational definitions, data collection plan, MSA/Gage R&R, baseline DPMO, sigma level, and capability.
- Analyze: Pareto, fishbone, 5 Whys, regression, ANOVA, hypothesis tests, and root cause verification.
- Improve: solution selection, DOE, pilots, mistake-proofing, risk assessment, and implementation plan.
- Control: control plan, SPC/control charts, standard work, response plan, training, and handoff checklist.

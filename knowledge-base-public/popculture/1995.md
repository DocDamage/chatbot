# 1995 Pop Culture and Major Stories

Domain: pop_culture
Year: 1995
Authority: canonical

## Canonical answer

A strong 1995 pop-culture reference is *Toy Story*. It made feature-length computer animation mainstream and signaled that CGI was not just a technical novelty but a new commercial and artistic lane for movies.

## Key facts

- *Toy Story* became the first fully computer-animated feature film.
- *Clueless* became a defining mid-1990s teen-comedy reference.
- Alanis Morissette's *Jagged Little Pill* helped define mainstream rock/pop in 1995.
- Windows 95 turned a software launch into a mass-market cultural event.
- PlayStation arrived in North America and helped reshape console gaming.

## Context

1995 sits at the hinge between analog 1990s media culture and the digital entertainment era: CGI film, CD-ROM/software hype, and 3D console gaming all moved closer to mainstream culture.

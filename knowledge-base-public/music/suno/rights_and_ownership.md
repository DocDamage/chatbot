# Suno Rights And Ownership

Always check Suno's current rights and ownership terms for the user account and intended use. Do not make false claims about commercial rights, royalty status, or ownership.

Guardrails:

- no copyrighted lyric continuation
- no living artist impersonation
- no exact artist cloning
- no unsupported ownership claims

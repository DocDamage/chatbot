# Suno Prompting

Use Suno prompts to describe genre, mood, instrumentation, vocal direction, section energy, and production finish. Prefer broad style traits over living-artist imitation.

Good prompt parts:

- genre and subgenre
- mood and tempo feel
- instrumentation
- vocal type and delivery
- structure and hook energy
- mix texture
- avoid list

Do not request copyrighted lyric continuation, exact artist cloning, or impersonation.

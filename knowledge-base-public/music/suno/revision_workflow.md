# Suno Revision Workflow

Revision prompts should say what to keep, what to change, and what to avoid.

Example:

Keep the hook melody and dark atmosphere. Make drums tighter, reduce vocal reverb in the verse, add a stronger final chorus, and avoid artist imitation.

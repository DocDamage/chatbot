# Logic Pro Vocal Chains

A stock Logic vocal chain can use Channel EQ, Compressor, DeEsser, optional saturation, reverb send, and delay send. Use Flex Pitch carefully for natural correction, not robotic overcorrection unless that is the artistic goal.
